About Us

Leading import and investment company in Australia

Your Gateway to Global Trade

Global Services Import & Investments is a premier import company based in Sydney, Australia, specializing in comprehensive logistics solutions and strategic investment opportunities. With over 15 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable import services across maritime, air, and land transportation.

Our expertise spans across multiple sectors including home products, construction materials, welding equipment, and electronics. We also provide expert guidance on stock market investments and project development, helping our clients maximize their returns in the Australian and international markets.
